A bit about me

My name is Lewis (Also known as 0x4248) and I am a software developer and cyber security enthusiast. I am currently studying Computer Science as a student.

I currently live in southern England. I have many other hobbies including radio DXing, photography. In my spare time I like to work on my own projects. I enjoy playing around with the Linux kernel and getting it to run on various devices.

I also enjoy doing 3D and VFX work in Blender. I have been using Blender for a few years and is one of my favorite pieces of software ever. I use it for everything to do with media even including video and image editing (Yes blender can do that).

Radio DXing

Just a little bit about my radio DXing hobby. I have been doing radio DXing for a few years now and I have a few radios that I use for this hobby. I mainly do amateur radio DXing and have picked many people up from around the world.

I have picked up people from

  • France
  • Germany
  • Italy
  • Spain
  • England
  • Finland
  • Switzerland
  • Canada (It even was a good signal)

I use a 2 meter dipole antenna which picks up signals very well. I mainly use the RTL-SDR V3. I also decode some signals (RTTY, Morse and FT-8). For my RTL-SDR I use SDR++ and for my decoding I use FLDIGI.
