Welcome

This site is designed primarily for desktop and laptop systems. Some content may not display optimally on mobile devices.

This is the personal website of 0x4248. It serves as a central location for my projects, research notes, experiments, and long-term technical work. Due to the recent downfall of the internet and the rise of heavy javascript frameworks, I wanted to create a site that lets me share my work in a fast, simple, and accessible way. Therefore this site is simple by design; low bandwith and low resource usage was a major goal.
Sadly this site isnt lynx, elinks, or any other text-based browser compatible, but any browser newer than 2010 should have no problem rendering it.

This website is heavily inspired by the old-school 2000-2010s web design which always gives me the nostalgic vibes of browsing the web in my early childhood. I always strive for simplicity and clear organization in my design, and I hope that this site reflects that. I also wanted to make something that

Take a scroll down to look at some of the things I have been working on recently, and feel free to explore the site using the navigation links on the left or top of the page. If you have any questions, suggestions, or just want to say hi, you can contact me at 0x4248@tuta.io.

Geo-blocking Notice

Due to ongoing political internet censorship and geo-blocking in certain regions, some users may experience difficulty accessing this site. This is common in countries with strict internet regulations (Including recently the UK) and may result in the site being completely inaccessible. If this is the case:

  • Use a VPN or proxy
  • Contact me 0x4248@tuta.com to report it with your country, ISP (Dont disclose more info than you are comfortable with) and any error messages you receive.
Many blocked websites are only small websites, forums, and even personal websites like this.

How do I navigate your site?

Navigation is intentionally simple. This site is in 3 main sections: notebook, nexus, and this page your on. I call this page the root pages.

Each section serves a distinct purpose:

  • Notebook - This is where I write notes, documentation, and anything i produce in Obsidian. Yes this page has way more JavaScript thats because its done using Quartz which is an amazing project.
  • Nexus - This is where almost all of my code lives. Its a simple, low-bandwidth git repository browser that I wrote myself.
  • Root - This page. It serves as a landing page and general introduction to the site. I also post news updates and general information about myself here.
