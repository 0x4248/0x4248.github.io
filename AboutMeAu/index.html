Radio DXing

Just a little bit about my radio DXing hobby. I have been doing radio DXing for a few years now and I have a few radios that I use for this hobby. I mainly do amateur radio DXing and have picked many people up from around the world.

I have picked up people from

  • France
  • Germany
  • Italy
  • Spain
  • England
  • Finland
  • Switzerland
  • Canada (It even was a good signal)

I have also picked up some interesting signals such as:

  • Russian buzzer
  • Time stations
  • Odd satellite signals passing over

I use a 2 meter dipole antenna which picks up signals very well. I mainly use the RTL-SDR V3. I also decode some signals (RTTY, Morse and FT-8). For my RTL-SDR I use SDR++ and for my decoding I use FLDIGI.
